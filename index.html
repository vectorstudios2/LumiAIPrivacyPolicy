Privacy Policy – Lumi AI

Effective Date: August 12, 2025

Vector Studios India (“we”, “our”, “us”) operates the mobile application Lumi AI (“App”). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and share information when you use Lumi AI.

1. Information We Collect

When you use Lumi AI, we collect the following information:

  • Conversations: The text you send and the AI’s responses.
  • Images: Any images you send to the App.

We do not collect your name, email, or other personal identifiers unless you voluntarily include them inside a conversation.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your conversations and images to process and respond to your requests and to generate AI responses.

3. Data Storage & Sharing

Your conversation text and images are stored locally on your device initially. When you send a message or image, the data is transmitted to our own AI API (operated by Vector Studios India) for processing. We may share the conversation and images with our AI processing services in order to provide responses.

We do not sell your data to advertisers or unrelated third parties.

4. Data Retention

We retain conversation text and images indefinitely. Currently, deletion requests are not supported and data cannot be removed on demand.

5. Children’s Privacy

Lumi AI is available for all age groups, including children. If you are under 18, please use the App with the consent and supervision of a parent or guardian.

6. Security

No special encryption or advanced security measures are implemented beyond standard operation of the App at this time.
